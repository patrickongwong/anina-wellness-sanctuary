01 The ANINA Idea

The wellness world is fragmented.
Your body is not.

A strength coach here. A physio across town. A yoga class on the weekend. A recovery spa when something finally breaks. Progress leaks out of the gaps between them.

ANINA is built on a single, quiet conviction: the systems that keep you strong, mobile and resilient are one system — so they should be trained as one. Every session braids mobility into strength, so range and load grow together instead of at each other's expense. One roof. One membership. One coherent plan for the next forty years.

  • Integrated, not fragmented — assessment, training, recovery in one place.
  • Mobility-infused strength — you get stronger through fuller range, not despite it.
  • Built for the long game — longevity as the outcome, not a buzzword.

02 Programs

Four pillars, one practice.

Nothing here is a standalone class you bolt on. Each pillar feeds the others — assessed together, programmed together, progressed together.

Pillar 01

Strength

Foundational, progressive resistance training — the single greatest lever for healthspan. Programmed to build force you can actually use.

Pillar 02

Mobility

Usable range at every joint, loaded and controlled. Not passive stretching — active capacity woven directly into your strength work.

Pillar 03

Longevity

Baseline assessment and periodic re-testing — strength, balance, capacity — so training is aimed at the metrics that predict a longer, abler life.

Pillar 04

Recovery

Sauna, mobility flushing and guided down-regulation. Recovery treated as part of the training — because adaptation happens when you rest.

03 The Space

A sanctuary, not a gym floor.

Warm wood, natural light, and room to breathe — designed to lower your shoulders the moment you walk in.
